Project Site Plan

Site Name

The Collective

This name represents a freelancer agency that gathers freelance workers connecting them to potential customers and vice-versa

Site Purpose

For The Collective freelancer agency

This site serves as a go between for freelancers and their prospective customers, it is mainly there to facilitate the hiring process while ensuring quality of work and preventing fraudulent behaviour on both sides

Scenarios

For the freelancers

  • What is the benefit of using this site?
  • How do I register?
  • How do I market my skills on this site?
  • Where can I contact support staff?

For the customers

  • What kind of service am I looking for?
  • How do I refine my search for freelancers?
  • Where ca I access a guided tour of the site?
